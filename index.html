The Forest of Old Spirts

While on the run from bandits, you enconter the Forest of Old Spirts. You notice little white figures running along with you. You've heard rumors that these white creatures are a sign of bad luck... but are they really?

You decide to run towards the white figures. The bandits also saw the white figures and ran away screaming. The little creatures lead you deep into the forest, and straight to a pond. How lucky! You're able to catch your breath and drink from the pond.

You decided to continue running. Sadly you have terrible stamina and the bandits ended up catching you. Maybe the bandits are nice and will let you go... Keep your spirts high and good luck may come!

As you are drinking from the pond you notice a figure. As you look closer, you notice that it's a forest spirt... The spirt is a giant wolf.The weirdly large and speaking wolf is actually really kind. Since you have no home to run back to, you decide to say with the wolf. The wolf spirt takes you back to her cave and you met the rest of her pack. How kind of her!

Turns out the bandits chased you down to get their stuff back. The stuff that you stoled... Anyway, good thing you have all of their stuff, right! right?? Sadly you don't and they throw you into a river, never to be found again. How upsetting.
